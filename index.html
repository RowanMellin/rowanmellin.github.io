About Me =)

 I began using art as an emotional outlet as soon as my undeveloped hand could grasp a pencil. In the context of art, I rarely let my brain run the show and shine the spotlight on my heart. My emotions, though overwhelming at times, bring my art to life. I will never stop being creative, my imagination is both a blessing and a curse. My art is a channel is which I am able to vent and in the process make pieces that hopefully others enjoy. My character is cut from a stubborn and tenacious cloth, I have a hard time giving up. My strength is derived from my own shortcomings and my worth reflects that.
